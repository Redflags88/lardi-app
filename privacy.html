Lardi School Management
Privacy Policy
How we collect, use and protect personal data · Last updated: May 2026
This system is operated by Leone Digital Africa Limited (trading as Lardi) on behalf of your school. All data collected belongs to the school. We process it only to provide the Lardi School Management System. We are registered with the Ghana Data Protection Commission under the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).
1. Who We Are

Lardi is a product of Leone Digital Africa Limited, operating under the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) of Ghana. We act as a data processor on behalf of your school, which is the data controller.

Contact: hello@lardigh.com · Accra, Ghana

2. What Personal Data We Collect
  • Students: Full name, date of birth, gender, class, home address
  • Parents/Guardians: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Staff: Full name, role, department, phone, email, subjects taught
  • Academic Records: Attendance, grades, term results, report cards
  • Financial Records: Fee payments and balances (no bank or card data)
  • System Users: Login email, role, access logs
3. Why We Collect This Data (Lawful Basis)
  • To perform the school management service under contract with the school
  • To enable teachers to mark attendance and record grades
  • To enable parents to view their child's academic progress
  • To enable the school to manage fee collection and financial reporting
  • To send school announcements and notices to the relevant audience
4. Data Minimality (Section 19, Act 843)

We only collect what is necessary to run the school management system. We do not collect health records, political opinions, religious beliefs, ethnicity, or any other special category data as defined under Section 37 of Act 843.

5. How We Protect Your Data (Section 28, Act 843)
  • All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) with encryption at rest and in transit
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Access is controlled by role-based authentication — each role sees only what is relevant
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Firebase security rules prevent unauthorised collection access
6. Who Has Access to the Data
  • School Admin: Full access to all school data
  • Teachers: Academic data only — no financial records
  • Parents: Only their own child's records
  • Students: Only their own timetable, grades and notices
  • Lardi: Technical access for system maintenance only
  • Google Firebase: Hosts the data under Google's data protection policies

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal data to any third party for any commercial purpose. This is prohibited under Sections 88 and 89 of Act 843.

7. Data Retention (Section 24, Act 843)

Student and academic data is retained for the duration of the school's subscription. When a school ends its subscription, all data is deleted within 30 days unless the school requests a data export before deletion.

8. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Data is stored on Google Firebase servers. This transfer is necessary to provide the cloud-based service. Google operates under internationally recognised data protection standards.

9. Your Rights as a Data Subject (Sections 32–35, Act 843)
  • Access — request to see the personal data held about you or your child
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — request that your data be deleted when no longer needed
  • Object — object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, contact your school admin directly. The school will respond within 40 days as required by Section 35(10) of Act 843.

10. Data Protection Commission Registration

Leone Digital Africa Limited (trading as Lardi) is registered with the Ghana Data Protection Commission as a data processor under the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).
